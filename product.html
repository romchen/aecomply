Guaranteed Peace of Mind

With your auto-enrolment employer's duties

All businesses with staff in the UK will soon find themselves subject to a wide range of new employer duties.

Your main duty as an employer will be to automatically enrol workers into a qualifying workplace pension scheme and to ensure that contributions from both the worker and the company are paid into the plan. æComplyLEX offers a comprehensive auto-enrolment package to ensure that all relevant workers are enrolled.

SAMPLE REPORT

Take a look at one of our FREE sample report to get an idea. download sample report

æComplyLEX aims to provide you, as an employer, with the necessary compliance understanding and support to assist you with your employer duties. This is achieved in three steps:

1 Preparation

The creation of a report, tailored to your specific circumstances. The report provides an overview of the new employer duties relevant to your business and information to assist you in making key preparations for your staging date and future obligations.

READ MORE >

2 Planning

The ability to financially model the direct costs of automatic enrolment, based on different scenarios and contribution profiles. This will allow your business to understand the financial implications of compliance.

READ MORE >

3 Ongoing Assistance

The ongoing assessment of your workforce, identifying actions which are required. This includes, for example, the automatic enrolment (and re-enrolment) of workers and communications which need to be issued to workers.

READ MORE >

You will need to make sure that you comply with these regulations at all times, not just at the staging date. æComplyLEX has been designed to make sure that you will achieve this on an ongoing basis.

æComplyLEX will help your business fulfill its regulatory responsibilities by:

  • Assessing qualifying earnings to determine which employees are eligible jobholders
  • Dealing with postponement periods
  • Preparing statutory notifications to workers
  • Managing actions such as ‘opt ins’ and ‘opt outs’
  • Communicating auto-enrolment actions back to the employer’s payroll
  • Triggering auto-enrolment of eligible employees
  • Allowing the employer to self-certify their chosen scheme(s) in line with regulatory requirements

Do you have multiple payroll periods?

æComplyLEX is able to assist you should you have multiple payroll periods (and/or several payroll providers), or where you have more than one pension scheme (even where those schemes are with different pension providers).
