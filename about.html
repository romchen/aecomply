Expert Advice You Can Trust

from leading pensions advisors and legal experts

æComplyLEX is an auto-enrolment solution provided by LEBC Group. LEBC has worked with Irwin Mitchell to include guidance on some of the legal and regulatory aspects of auto-enrolment into æComplyLEX.

LEBC Group Limited: Established in 2000, LEBC is a national IFA pensions and employee benefits consultancy with 14 branches and over 140 dedicated staff.

Auto-enrolment advice and services

In addition to æComplyLEX, LEBC have created services to help your organisation comply with the whole range of employer responsibilities and to achieve its aims in relation to auto-enrolment.

Detailed pension schemes assessment

LEBC will help you carry out an audit of your pension arrangements. Any existing scheme will require some level of health check to ensure that it meets all qualifying criteria and that it is suitable for your workforce.

Visit the LEBC website

Irwin Mitchell: Irwin Mitchell’s solicitors offer expert legal advice and representation on a range of issues. A top twenty, full service national law firm, Irwin Mitchell has a network of 9 offices and employs over 2,200 people.

Auto-enrolment advice and services

Irwin Mitchell has advised many clients on satisfying their new employer duties. Businesses may require advice in relation to how auto-enrolment interacts with contractual enrolment processes or salary sacrifice arrangements, or in relation to any specific contractual entitlements of certain employees. Where amendments to employment contracts may be required, Irwin Mitchell can advise on the relevant issues.
