Your business, your pension scheme.

All employers with staff in the UK will soon be required to enter certain workers into a qualifying pension scheme.
æComplyplus is a comprehensive compliance tool, which aims to help your business:

BE COMPLIANT

The employer duties are extensive and evolving. Using æComplyplus will keep you up-to-date with the latest changes to auto-enrolment as well as identifying ongoing requirements.

SAVE TIME

With access to automated processes, template communications and information management tools, æComplyplus will allow you to comply with your auto-enrolment requirements in an efficient manner, reducing the administrative burden on your business.

AVOID FINES

Failure to comply with the employer duties can, in certain circumstances, lead to fixed penalties and fines of up to £10,000 per day. By identifying, explaining and assisting with required actions, æComplyplus will help you to avoid non-compliance.

Auto-Enrolment Staging Dates

The Pensions Regulator recommends allowing 12 months to prepare for auto-enrolment. You should prepare immediately if you are within 6 months of your staging date. æComplyplus will assist you in identifying and completing the necessary preparation.

Your “staging date” is effectively the date upon which key employer duties are switched on for your business. *Staging dates are generally based on the size of an employer’s PAYE scheme as at 1 April 2012. However, there are certain exceptions.

FAQs

What is an jobholder?

The Pensions Regulator defines a jobholder as a worker who is aged between 16 and 74, is working or ordinarily works in the UK under their contract and has qualifying earnings.
