Your business, your pension scheme.

All UK employers are now required to assess all their workers and where required, enrol them into a qualifying workplace pension scheme. æComplyLEX helps your business:

BE COMPLIANT

The employer duties are extensive and evolving. Using æComplyLEX will keep you up-to-date with the latest changes to auto-enrolment as well as identifying what you need to do and any ongoing requirements.

SAVE TIME

With access to automated processes, template communications and information management tools, æComplyLEX will allow you to comply with your auto-enrolment requirements in an efficient manner, reducing the administrative burden on your business.

AVOID FINES

Failure to comply with the employer duties can, in certain circumstances, lead to fixed penalties and fines of up to £10,000 per day. By identifying, explaining and assisting with required actions, æComplyLEX will help you to avoid non-compliance.

Forthcoming staging dates

Plan ahead: The Pensions Regulator recommends allowing 12 months to prepare for auto-enrolment. You should prepare immediately if you are within 6 months of your staging date. æComplyLEX will assist you in identifying and completing the necessary preparation for your staging date and remaining compliant thereafter.

Your staging date is effectively the date upon which key employer duties are switched on for your business. *Staging dates are generally based on the size of an employer’s PAYE scheme as at 1 April 2012. However, there are certain exceptions.

FAQs

What is an jobholder?

The Pensions Regulator defines a jobholder as a worker who is aged between 16 and 74, is working or ordinarily works in the UK under their contract and has qualifying earnings.
