AUTO-ENROLMENT PREPARATION

æComplyLEX is an online automatic enrolment compliance tool which, unlike many other products, also addresses the key legal issues raised by your new employer duties.

After answering a questionnaire relating to your workforce and existing pension arrangements, we will generate a tailored report designed to help you prepare for auto-enrolment and understand it works.

This report will also identify some potential issues which will need to be addressed, both prior to your staging date for automatic enrolment and beyond.

This report will help you prepare for the following:

Key duties

A clear explanation and summary of the key duties you will have to comply with.

Staging Date

Information in relation to the staging date applicable to your business.

Your existing pension arrangements

The report aims to guide you on the following:

  • Amendments which may be required to existing arrangements
  • The interaction between automatic enrolment and salary sacrifice arrangements (and/or flexible benefits) as well as contractual enrolment

The impact on your workforce

You will need to consider issues such as:

  • Promises in a worker’s contract relating to their pension provision, which may differ from their entitlements under the auto-enrolment regime
  • Treatment of agency workers, volunteers and workers on secondment
  • Workers who are ordinarily working within the UK and dealing with international workers
  • Treatment of office holders (non-executive directors and trustees), contractors and consultants
  • Inducements and prohibited recruitment conduct

Action plan

The report will suggest some key actions for your business to be undertaken in preparation for your staging date, as well as issues you may need to address in further detail with your advisers.

Glossary of key terms

A list of common terms, demystifying the jargon of automatic enrolment.

You can re-take the questionnaire at any time to alter your answers if the circumstances of your business change. This ensures that the guidance received in the report remains appropriate. The report will also be updated as and when legislation changes, allowing you to stay on top of any legal developments.
